About the Project

Atramentous is a data-driven platform designed to identify habitable worlds beyond our solar system. By leveraging complex planetary data and machine learning algorithms, we analyze physical and orbital characteristics to predict the likelihood of life-sustaining environments.

This project utilizes extensive datasets provided by NASA, including the Exoplanet Archive and Kepler mission observations, to ensure the highest level of scientific accuracy in our predictions.
